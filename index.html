About Us

We are a student activity at Faculty of Engineering, Ain Shams University. Our goal is to strengthen and revitalize the students of the Computer, communication & mechatronics Departments in different tracks.

Additionally, we aim to enhance members technical and soft skills to meet labor market requirements through creating a fun learning environment for our members (or anyone interested in their disciplines!)
By preparing workshops, collaborative projects and hackathons.

  • Available Tracks on 2023
  • Embedded systems
  • Artificial Intelligence (AI)
  • Mobile Development
  • Desktop Application Development
  • Competitive Programming
